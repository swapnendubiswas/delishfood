🥪 Veg Sandwich

⬅ Back to Recipes

📝 Ingredients

  • Bread Slices
  • Tomato, Cucumber, Onion (sliced)
  • Butter, Green Chutney
  • Salt, Pepper

👨‍🍳 Steps

  1. Apply butter and chutney on bread slices.
  2. Layer with veggies, sprinkle salt & pepper.
  3. Toast or grill if desired. Serve fresh.
