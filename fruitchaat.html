🍎 Fruit Chaat

⬅ Back to Recipes

📝 Ingredients

  • Apple, Banana, Orange, Pomegranate
  • Lemon Juice - 1 tbsp
  • Chaat Masala - 1 tsp

👨‍🍳 Steps

  1. Chop all fruits into bite-sized pieces.
  2. Add lemon juice and chaat masala.
  3. Mix well and serve chilled.
