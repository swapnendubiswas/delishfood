🍛 Chicken Biryani

⬅ Back to Recipes

📝 Ingredients

  • Chicken - 500g (marinated)
  • Basmati Rice - 2 cups
  • Onions - 2 (sliced)
  • Yogurt - 1/2 cup
  • Biryani Masala, Ginger-Garlic Paste, Salt

👨‍🍳 Steps

  1. Marinate chicken with yogurt, spices, and ginger-garlic paste.
  2. Cook rice till 70% done. Keep aside.
  3. Fry onions, add marinated chicken, and cook till done.
  4. Layer rice over chicken, cover, and dum cook for 15 minutes.
  5. Garnish with coriander and serve hot.
