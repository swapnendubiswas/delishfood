🥗 Healthy Salad

⬅ Back to Recipes

📝 Ingredients

  • Lettuce, Cucumber, Tomato, Carrot
  • Olive Oil - 1 tbsp
  • Lemon Juice - 1 tbsp
  • Salt & Pepper - to taste

👨‍🍳 Steps

  1. Wash and chop all vegetables.
  2. Mix in a large bowl.
  3. Drizzle olive oil and lemon juice, add salt & pepper.
  4. Toss well and serve fresh.
