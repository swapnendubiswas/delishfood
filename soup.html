🍅 Tomato Soup

⬅ Back to Recipes

📝 Ingredients

  • Tomatoes - 4
  • Onion - 1, Garlic - 2 cloves
  • Butter - 1 tbsp
  • Salt, Pepper, Water

👨‍🍳 Steps

  1. Boil and peel tomatoes. Blend into puree.
  2. Saute onion and garlic in butter.
  3. Add puree, salt, pepper, and water. Simmer 10 mins.
  4. Serve hot with croutons.
