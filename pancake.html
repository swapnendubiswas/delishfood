🥞 Banana Pancakes

⬅ Back to Recipes

📝 Ingredients

  • Bananas - 2 (ripe)
  • Flour - 1 cup
  • Milk - 1/2 cup
  • Egg - 1
  • Sugar - 2 tbsp, Baking Powder - 1 tsp

👨‍🍳 Steps

  1. Mash bananas in a bowl.
  2. Add egg, milk, sugar, flour, and baking powder. Mix to make a batter.
  3. Heat pan, pour batter, cook both sides till golden brown.
  4. Serve hot with honey or syrup.
