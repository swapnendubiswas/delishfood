🍝 Cheesy Pasta

⬅ Back to Recipes

📝 Ingredients

  • Pasta - 1 cup
  • Cheese - 1/2 cup (grated)
  • Milk - 1/2 cup
  • Butter - 1 tbsp
  • Salt & Pepper - to taste

👨‍🍳 Steps

  1. Boil pasta in salted water and drain.
  2. In a pan, melt butter and add milk.
  3. Add cheese, stir until melted.
  4. Mix in pasta and season with salt & pepper.
  5. Serve hot with garnish!
